ABC Library Database

Description

ABC Library is located in a small community and have decided to develop a database to store information electronically. The Library collection consists of various items. The items could be of three types; Books, CD’s, and DVD’s and multiple copies are kept for popular items. Sometimes Books would also have copies of older editions available. Each individual copy of an item would have a unique barcode to distinguish it from others.
