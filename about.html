Career Goals

Since a computer appeared in front of my eyes, I have always wanted to know what is hidden behind a monitor, a keyboard and a CPU. My interest in computer science began at a very young age. I was and I am still amazed of what can be done through the power of a simple application.

Biography

Alejandro Mancebo is a current Computer Programming college student who believes in producing a high quality of code taking efficient approaches based on users’ inputs with the objective of gaining customer satisfaction. He specializes in full-stack development ensuring the responsiveness of applications using a range of different technologies and languages such as Java, C#, HTML, CSS, PHP, ASP.NET, JavaScript, MySQL, React, Angular and Microsoft Visual Studio.

Alejandro’s professional life started more than 10 years ago when he graduated in the Mechanical Engineering field in Cuba. As an Engineer, he worked in different industries and roles from a supervisor in a hardware depot to Quality Specialist in the Cuban National Power Plant Company. In addition to implementing and managing Quality Management Systems and coordinating production activities, Alejandro designed relational databases to store all the quality control procedures and project-related documentation.

When moving to Canada he had the opportunity to study Computer Numeric Controlled (CNC) programming which combines the ability to read and interpret technical blueprints, some mechanical aptitude, and strong attention to detail with programming. But, while was working as a CNC programmer he noticed that in North America in other to move your business forward you need to be online and therefore there is a high demand for computer programmers. With that in mind, Alejandro recognized he needed more tools to advance his professional career and enrolled in the Computer Programming program at George Brown.
