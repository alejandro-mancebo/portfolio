Residence Inventory

Description

A custom inventory management system designed to maintain the storage of stock, control the amount of products on storage and order fulfillment. It also keeps track of the residence's goods through acquisition.

The Residence Inventory has helped owners to make decisions about ordering, and to keep track of inventory, optimize fulfilment, reduce costs and prevent losses due to theft or spoilage.

Features

  • Check items in or out
  • Generate dynamic inventory reports based on different search criteria
  • Filter data based on particular criteria
  • Export reports to CSV files
  • Allow staff instant access to the system
  • Get a warning message when the stock is low and the product expiry date is approaching
  • Provide information about the product brand, price, check-in date, and expiry date, category, and quantity.

Technology: Microsoft Access, Visual Basic for Applications (VBA) and Microsoft Excel
